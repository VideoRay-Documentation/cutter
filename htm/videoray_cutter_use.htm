VideoRay Cutter Operating Tips

The Cutter is easy to mount and use.

Hardware Set Up

  1. Mount the inner clamp on the skid using the supplied screws.

  2. Place the cutter in the clamp and install the outer clamp using the supplied screws.

  3. Remove the 9 Pin Accessory Port Dummy Plug from the Accessory Port.

  4. Install the 9 Pin connector from the Cutter into the Accessory Port.

Operation

Use the buttons on the hand controller to open or close the cutter.

The manipulator instrument will indicate the status of the cutter activity.

The Pro 4 cutter is not compatible with the Pro 3 and vice versa. Do not attempt to plug the cutter into an incompatible ROV.

This equipment is optional and may not be included in all VideoRay Pro 4 configurations.
